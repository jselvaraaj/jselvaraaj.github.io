---

my broad research interests focus on advancing autonomous systems and robotics by making them: 1. more robust, 2. better at specific tasks, and 3. closer to general-purpose functionality. To that end, I am exploring how deep learning/deep RL as a tool be combined with control theory and physics. 

at umass, i studied applying neural differential equations for episode trajectory modeling of grid environment as part of my undergrad honors thesis with prof. bruno castro da silva.
